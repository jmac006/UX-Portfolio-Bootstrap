Heads Up! - A Beer Rating App Case Study

Heads Up! is an app developed to provide users the opportunity to search for a specific beer based on their preferences. This helps our users find the perfect beer every time!

Team Members: Amy Kim, Justin Mac, Maricela Austgen

Tools Used: Adobe XD, MIRO, Google Tools, Vecteezy
Role: UX/UI Designer (25% Research, 45% UI Design, 30% User Testing)
Time Frame: 3 weeks

Problem Statement

The Problem: Many beer drinkers are often annoyed when their favorite beer option isn't available for purchase and hate it when they end up picking the wrong beer to drink. People are overwhelmed by the amount of options they have to choose from.

The Solution: Heads up! allows people to search, rate, and purchase beers in an all-in-one beer app to make an informed decision about their next beer purchase.

User Research

Our interview questions and answers validated our theory that people typically have a hard time choosing a new beer to try. We wanted to find out with userâ€™s would be interested in an app that would help them discover new beers to help aid them in the beer-buying decision process.

After our interviews, we found that the majority of interviewees enjoy beer, but have a difficult time choosing a new beer to try. In addition we asked if they would be interested in using an app that would help them get quick and valuable information to help them decide, and 92.5% said they would be interested in utilizing an app.

Key Learnings for User Tests

  • Users wanted to view more information on the home screen
  • Camera button: large and in the center of the tab bar
  • Users clicked on the stars to view the ratings, rather than the button
  • Users valued reading reviews vs leaving a review
  • Improved upon legibility by changing colors
  • Users didn't feel comfortable with uploading their photo ID. So we decided to dig deeper into other apps and websites that sell alcohol and found we only needed to verify that the user is 21+.
